About Us

NewCo Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit driven by the incubation of ideas, products, services, and companies founded by or employing students.

Our Work

We support on and off campus programs that provide employment opportunities for high school and college students to make meaningful contributions to startup endeavors.

We provide Back-Office Support Services for School-Based Enterprise (BOSS for SBE) to enable and streamline on-campus businesses in career education programs.

BOSS Application

Our Impact

We accelerate readiness for college and career by means of an entrepreneurial apprenticeship model. In addition to the free BOSS for SBE offering, we provide student internships, faculty stipends, event sponsorships, mentor development, school equipment and supplies.
